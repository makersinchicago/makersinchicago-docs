COVID-19 Recommendations

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Makers in Chicago recommends healthy individuals able to make PPE (3D Printers, Laser Cutters, Sewing Machines, etc) sign up and join the Illinois PPE Network.

For individuals with computers but without the ability to make PPE, Makers in Chicago recommends downloading the Folding@home tool and donating your computer's proccessing power to their work.
