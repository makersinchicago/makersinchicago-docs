COVID-19 Recommendations

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Makers in Chicago recommends individuals with digital fabrication tools (3D Printers, Laser Cutters, etc) create parts for building face shields requested by Swedish Covenant Hospital. The Chief Medical Officer at Swedish has approved designs from Limitless Studios found on COVIDSupplyConnection.com. If you are interested in fabricating parts to donate, join their Facebook Group, 3D Printed Face Shields for Swedish, read the About page daily, and take part in the discussion. Swedish has pledged to distribute the shields to healthcare workers across Chicago.

For individuals with computers but without digital fabrication tools, Makers in Chicago recommends downloading the Folding@home tool and donating your computer's proccessing power to their work.
